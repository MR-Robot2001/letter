Privacy Policy

Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our website [website URL] and our services.

1. Information We Collect We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, address, email, and any details related to your orders. We also collect data about your use of our website, like your IP address and browser type.

2. How We Use Your Information We use your information to: Process and send your orders. Communicate with you about your orders and our services. Improve our website and services.

3. Automated Processing All information you provide is processed automatically. Your data is printed and sealed without being read by any human.

4. Data Retention We do not save any data longer than necessary. All personal information is automatically erased 10 days after your order is processed.

5. Sharing Your Information We only share your information with: Service providers who help us operate our business (like payment processors and delivery services). Authorities if required by law.

6. Data Security We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights Since we do not retain your information longer than 10 days, there is no ongoing personal data to access, update, or delete after this period.

8. Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us
